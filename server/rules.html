Rules

Server Rules

  1. Please keep the chat clean. This includes spamming (any message repeated four times counts as spam and will result in a temporary mute), advertising (don't advertise things which are not related to the server), and non-roleplay swearing.
  2. Do not use hacks, cheats and glitches. A hack is defined as a client or mod which will give you an unfair advantage over other players, and may result in a ban.
  3. Don't scam other players or attempt to steal their personal info or login.
  4. Do not hesitate asking help or submitting propositions in #suggestions. The ones that get a sufficient amount of votes will be used if staff agree, or will get submitted in #polls if staff don't agree.
  5. No alt accounts are permitted. Using one will result in a warning and the alt account will be banned.

Roleplay Rules

  1. Your skin must stay the same until you die a total death.
  2. When a character dies a "total death," the new character doesn't know anything about the life of the first character. However, he/she can learn it from other players.
  3. The maximum amount of times your player can die a total death in a week is one time.
  4. Swearing is fine in roleplay, but abusing of it is not allowed.
  5. Scamming other players (in roleplay, not in real life) is allowed, but abusing of it is not. You are forbidden to scam others for gold ingots.
  6. Sexual consent is okay, but only in roleplay. Do not abuse it, or you will be instantly banned.
  7. Harassment is okay in roleplay. However, it has to stay in roleplay. Abusing of it can result in a warn.
  8. Staff members are also playing. They are not allowed to use their rank to cheat. Report any staff who are abusing their rank.
  9. Don't try to find loopholes in these rules, abusing it will not go unpunished.
