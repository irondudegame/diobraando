About the Server

Dio Braando is a Semi-RP server hosted by Minehut. It was started by Totolapin (JoJo'bik) in mid-July, 2020. Within a week, it already had a staff team, who helped build, manage, and develop the server. Currently, it is in Season 1.

Stats

Stats can also be gained using special potions. A Ⅰ potion will increase your stat by 0.1. A Ⅱ potion will increase your stat by 0.2.

Strength

The strength stat is a stat that is used to increase attack damage on entities. To get 0.1 strength, you must have 10 strength points. You recieve 4 strength points for every player you kill, and 1 strength point for every entity you kill. The maximum amount of strength you can have is 10, with the exception of orcs, who can have up to 12.

Resistance

The resistance stat is used to reduce the amount of damage dealt to you. To get 0.1 resistance, you must have 5 resistance (or res) points. You recieve 1 res point every time you take 4 hearts of damage. The maximum amount of strnegth you can have is 10, with the exception of dwarves, who can have up to 14.

Archery

The archery stat increases the power of your bow and the chance that you will fire two arrows. To gain 0.1 of the archery stat, you must have 5 archery points. Each time you kill an entity using an arrow, you get 1 archery point. The maximum amount of the archery stat you can have is 10.

Other Stats

The magic stat and speed stats are still in development. You can suggest something in #skript-suggestions.

Creatures

Werewolf

The werewolf has a 40% chance of spawning instead of a wolf, but only at night. The werewolf will attack all types of players, excluding players who have turned into werewolves, and cannot be tamed. If you are attacked by a werewolf, you will become a werewolf for 20 seconds, and you will be unable to control yourself and attack other players.

Magic

Wands

There are 3 types of wands obtainable -- the human wand (enchanted stick), the orc wand (enchanted bone), and the dark wand (enchanted blaze rod). They each have specific spells that you can learn and use.

Spells

Spells can be learned from spellbooks, which may be found hidden in bookshelves. To select which spell you want to use, type /ss [spell]. To list all the spells you know, type /spellbook. Wands may be found in some secret locations.

  • [Human] Fireball: Shoot a fireball from your wand.
  • [Human] Snowstorm: Shoot 4 snowballs from your wand.
  • [Orc] Evocation: Summon an evocation fang under the victim's feet.
  • [Orc] Heal: Heal all non-human players within 10 blocks, but damage all humans.
  • [Dark] Soul cast: Shoot a spell that will damage the victim by a specific amount of health, depending on stats.
  • [Dark] Necromancy: Only castable on a target with less than 4 hearts, heals them but gives them poison for 100 seconds, and has all stats divided by 2 until 100 seconds pass.
